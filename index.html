About MDHL

Fostering excellence in youth hockey for over 25 years

The Mighty Ducks Hockey League (MDHL) was established in 1996 to provide young athletes with a structured, competitive environment to develop their hockey skills while learning valuable life lessons.


We believe that hockey is more than just a sport‚Äîit's a platform for building character, teamwork, and lifelong friendships. Our league serves players aged 4-16 across all skill levels, from beginners to advanced competitors.


With experienced coaches, state-of-the-art facilities, and a commitment to fair play, MDHL has become one of the premier youth hockey organizations in the region.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, inclusive, and competitive environment where young athletes can develop their hockey skills, build character, and create lasting memories while fostering sportsmanship, teamwork, and personal growth.

League Rules & Guidelines

Ensuring fair play and safety for all participants

1

Eligibility

All players must be between 4-16 years old and have a signed parental consent form. Medical clearance is required for all participants.

2

Equipment

Full protective gear is mandatory: helmet with face mask, shoulder pads, elbow pads, gloves, hockey pants, shin guards, and skates. All equipment must meet league safety standards.

3

Conduct

Players, coaches, and spectators must demonstrate respect and sportsmanship at all times. Zero tolerance for fighting, verbal abuse, or unsportsmanlike conduct.

4

Attendance

Regular attendance at practices and games is expected. Players must notify their coach at least 24 hours in advance of any absence.

5

Fair Play

All players must receive equal ice time regardless of skill level. Emphasis is on development and enjoyment of the game.

6

Game Day

Players must arrive 30 minutes before game time. Team colors must be worn. Late arrivals may result in reduced playing time.
